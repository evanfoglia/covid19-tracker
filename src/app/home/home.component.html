How do I prevent myself from getting COVID-19?

There's currently no cure or vaccine for the COVID-19. The best possible way to prevent yourself from contracting the virus is to avoid gatherings of larger than 10 people and wearing a mask at all times while indoor.

What are the symptoms of COVID-19?

The most common COVID-19 symptoms are fevers or chills, cough, shortness of breath, and fatigue.
